World of Warcraft Class Descriptions

Warrior

Fearless front-line fighters who rely on brute strength, heavy armor, and rage to deal and absorb damage.

Paladin

Holy knights who combine melee combat with divine magic to protect allies, heal wounds, and smite evil.

Hunter

Ranged masters with loyal beasts at their side, hunters specialize in tracking, traps, and precision ranged attacks.

Rogue

Stealthy assassins who strike from the shadows using poisons, cunning, and high burst damage.

Priest

Priests wield holy or shadow magic to heal allies or punish enemies depending on their specialization.

Death Knight

Once servants of the Lich King, they wield unholy powers and command the dead with devastating effect.

Shaman

Elemental wielders who call upon the power of earth, fire, wind, and water to aid their allies or defeat foes.

Mage

Masters of arcane, fire, and frost magic, mages dominate with ranged spell power and battlefield control.

Warlock

Fel magic users who summon demons and drain the life force from their enemies.

Monk

Martial artists who harness chi for rapid attacks, healing, and defensive techniques.

Druid

Shape-shifters who channel nature’s power to heal, deal damage, or defend in multiple roles.

Demon Hunter

Agile warriors infused with fel energy who strike fast with glaives and leap across the battlefield.

Evoker

Dracthyr-exclusive spellcasters that harness the magic of dragonflights for mid-range support or damage.
